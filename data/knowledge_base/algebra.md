# Algebra (Comprehensive - JEE Scope)

## Quadratic Equations
For $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ ($a \ne 0$):
- **Roots**: $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a}$ where $D = b^2 - 4ac$.
- **Nature of Roots**:
  - $D > 0$: Real and distinct.
  - $D = 0$: Real and equal.
  - $D < 0$: Complex conjugate pair.
- **Sum of Roots**: $\alpha + \beta = -\frac{b}{a}$
- **Product of Roots**: $\alpha \beta = \frac{c}{a}$

## Sequences & Series
### Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- $n$-th term: $a_n = a + (n-1)d$
- Sum of $n$ terms: $S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d]$

### Geometric Progression (GP)
- $n$-th term: $a_n = ar^{n-1}$
- Sum of $n$ terms: $S_n = \frac{a(r^n - 1)}{r - 1}$ ($r \ne 1$)
- Infinite GP Sum: $S_{\infty} = \frac{a}{1-r}$ ($|r| < 1$)

## Permutations & Combinations
- $^nP_r = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
- $^nC_r = \binom{n}{r} = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$
- Properties: $^nC_r = ^nC_{n-r}$

## Binomial Theorem
- $(x+y)^n = \sum_{r=0}^n \binom{n}{r} x^{n-r} y^r$
- Number of terms: $n+1$

## Complex Numbers
- $z = x + iy$
- Modulus: $|z| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$
- Argument: $\theta = \tan^{-1}(y/x)$
- Euler's Form: $z = r e^{i\theta}$
- Cube Roots of Unity ($1, \omega, \omega^2$):
  - $1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0$
  - $\omega^3 = 1$
